Step-by-Step Technical Instructions

To effectively minimize the AI's presence, you need to dig into the hidden menus within the mobile and desktop applications. Although Meta does not provide a single "Disable All AI" button, you can achieve a similar effect by muting specific interactions and resetting your preference data.

1. Disabling AI-Enhanced Search

Go to your Facebook Account Settings, then navigate to "Media and Features." Under the "Search Preferences" tab, look for "AI Assistance." By turning this toggle off, you effectively revert your search bar to its legacy state, ensuring that your queries lead to people and posts rather than AI-generated essays.

Pro Strategy

If you find that the AI returns even after a logout, try clearing your "Search History" in the Privacy Center. This forces the algorithm to stop predicting your interests based on previous AI interactions.

2. Managing the Chatbot Presence

If Meta AI has started a conversation with you in your inbox, don't ignore it—mute it. Long-press the chat and select "Mute Notifications" indefinitely. This prevents the AI from popping up with "helpful" suggestions while you are trying to message your friends or family.
